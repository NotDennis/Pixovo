Who Are We?

We are a local website design company located in the Greater Philadelphia Region in Pennsylvania.

Our team consists of highly passionate high school students who are committed to making low cost, awesome websites for small businesses and organizations.

What Do We Do?

Our main priority is to deliver low-cost, attractive websites for local businesses in order to help their businesses, especially during the COVID-19 pandemic.

Using a variety of languages, platforms, and plugins, we design anything from nonprofit websites to small restaurant websites, among others.

What Do We Use To Design Our Websites?

We use a wide variety of platforms and languages to design websites. However, the preferred platform of choice is Wordpress.

In the past, we have used HTML, CSS, Javascript, Wordpress, and Wix to build our websites. However, regardless of what we use we strive to make the best, cleanest websites.

How Long Do Projects Take To Complete?

The length of time to complete a website depends on the size and complexity of the project. For smaller scale projects, the time required can be anywhere from 24 hours to 72 hours, and for larger projects, the time required can be anywhere from a couple of days to a week.

Please contact us 3-4 days in advance to brief us on the type and components of the website you want.

How Much Do Our Websites Cost?

Depending on the type of website the client wants, custom-made sites will generally cost around $10. However, the cost may increase with added features or functionalities.

Web hosting is free, although please remember a custom domain name can cost anywhere from $10 ~ $15.

What is Our Refund Policy?

Keep in mind pricing is very negotiable! We have a 3-part payment system. We ask for a third of the full payment upfront.

Then, around halfway into designing the website, we ask for the second third of the payment if you are satisfied. If not, we will refund the full amount you payed us in the beginning.

If you are satisfied, we will finish the website and ask for your review. If you are not satisfied with the entire website, we will offer a half refund of the payment we have received. If you are satisfied, then we ask for the remaining payment after the website is officially launched.








All our transactions are done through PayPal.
